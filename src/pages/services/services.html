Academics

Our vision as a department is to empower the pupils both academically and spiritually through integrating Biblical principles into our teaching. We aim at equipping each pupil with sound knowledge and skills that will enable them to solve problems as well as deal and cope with the technical aspect of their lives. It is our hope that by the time pupils leave high school, they are familiar with the technological developments in their subject areas.

Overview

Dadaya High School has a vibrant and dynamic Science Department. We are very proud of the achievements of our students. Some of the high lights are that in 2014 two of our students came first and second in the Science Olympiad and in 2015 a number came in the top ten students. WE have also achieved excellent results in the Cambridge examinations with a number of top students in Zimbabwe.

Overview

The Humanities Department is one of the largest Departments at Gateway High School, with 10 teachers, of which 5 are females. Our department is housed in the heart of the main quad building adjacent to the Arts Department. In most cases we register between 90 to 100% pass-rate at both A and O Levels.



Lessons in the Humanities Department offer very much more than skills related to the subject area or the workplace. It is one of the best ways of preparing students for critical citizenship in a rapidly changing world, a world that demands versatility and creativity rather than mere teaching and learning. The students are also prepared for university entrance especially in the following areas> the Arts, the Social Sciences and will ensure that the student’s journey leads to personal growth and intellectual development that will change his/her life for the better. In a nutshell, Humanities Department prepares students for university entrance and most of our products in particular, occupy cutting-edge positions in industry, the media, government and virtually all sectors of the economy. Our students are recognised by the world’s universities and employers.
